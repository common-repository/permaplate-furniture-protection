== Description ==
PermaPlate Furniture Protection Plan allows merchants to offer furniture service contracts online within a few hours.   
 
##overview	
 **PermaPlate Furniture Protection** was created with the mission to redesign the furniture warranty experience for customers. Our service to furniture retailers (and their customers) reflects a fresh perspective of how warranty services should be administered. Innovation and solutions to our furniture retailers include a wider perspective, which will enhance your customer’s long-term experience.

 **PermaPlate Furniture Protection** took a wider perspective that reshaped how our furniture warranty services will be delivered by 

  - Understanding consumer behavior and the needs in the market.
  - Building underwriting guidelines that produce higher customer scores.
  - Eliminating unnecessary claim barriers that frustrate customers.
  - Leveraging technologies to decrease the time to resolve furniture issues.

We look forward to the opportunity to help grow your business and service the needs of your trusted furniture customers. Provide your customers with straightforward, hassle-free protection plans that will increase engagement, growth, and profitability.

With QuickCover® technology, the  **PermaPlate Furniture Protection** Plan allows merchants to offer furniture service contracts online within a few hours. QuickCover® provides fast, seamless integration of service contract sales online.

##Account Setup
 You can quickly and easily deploy the PermaPlate Furniture Protection 
plan using a plugin and direct eCommerce integrations. Start selling PermaPlate Furniture Protection plans today!

1. Download and install the **PermaPlate Furniture Protection** plugin.
2. Sign up for a QuickCover® account at [ https://signup.quickcover.me/join/permaplate]( https://signup.quickcover.me/join/permaplate/) to enable API services, map product categories and obtain pricing.
3. Sync your store product catalog to the QuickCover® product categories to enable sales of **PermaPlate Furniture Protection** plans.

For every plan you sell, you capture the revenue upfront and we will bill you the merchant price weekly. Please refer to additional set-up content and full documentation [here](https://docs.quickcover.me/)
